## ◆ PHILOSOPHY ◆

```
    ╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   "ASCII art is often dismissed as nostalgia or novelty.     ║
    ║    This museum argues otherwise."                            ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   When constrained to monospace characters, creativity       ║
    ║   flourishes differently. Every line, dot, character         ║
    ║   becomes intentional. The medium imposes discipline—        ║
    ║   and discipline reveals essence.                            ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   These aren't "pictures made of text." They're:             ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   • Terminal-native aesthetics that belong in code           ║
    ║   • Visual cyphers that communicate beyond language          ║
    ║   • Abstract patterns that reveal structure                  ║
    ║   • Art that scales from tiny terminals to massive displays  ║
    ║   • Universal symbols readable across cultures               ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   In a world of images, emojis, and GIFs—there remains       ║
    ║   something profound about art composed of the same          ║
    ║   characters that write our code, documentation, and         ║
    ║   thoughts.                                                  ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ║   This is art native to the medium of text itself.           ║
    ║                                                               ║
    ╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```
