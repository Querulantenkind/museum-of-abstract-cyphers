───────────────────────────────[ COMBINATION RULES ]───────────────────────────────

1. **Primary Structure Rule**  
   Use FAMILY A to define macro-geometry (frames, quadrants, corridors).

2. **Density Modulation Rule**  
   Populate interior zones with FAMILY B.  
   Higher density required → choose @ then # then % as fallback strata.

3. **Void Protocol**  
   When a void-core is declared:  
     Replace entire subregion with FAMILY C (lightest possible marks).  
   Nesting voids permitted *only* if parent void uses larger radius.

4. **Spiral Synthesis Rule**  
   FAMILY D elements must be placed in either:
     • monotonically rotating arcs, or  
     • logarithmic contractions toward a focal point.  
   Never mix < and > without a turning pivot.

5. **Noise-Phase Overlay Rule**  
   FAMILY E may be **superposed** over B or D zones to simulate grain.  
   Ratio guideline:  
     stable region → 5% noise  
     turbulent region → 20–40% noise  
     storm apex → 60%+

6. **Fractal Tiling Rule**  
   Any region may be recursively replaced by a scaled copy of its own
   four-quadrant subdivision, as long as:
     - Frame width shrinks proportionally  
     - Void-cores scale discontinuously  
     - Spiral vectors preserve handedness
