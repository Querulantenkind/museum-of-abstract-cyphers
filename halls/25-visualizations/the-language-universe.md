║                                    LANGUAGE ACQUISITION                                                                    ║
║                              How Children Learn Their First Language                                                       ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║    Age          Milestone                           Example                                                               ║
║  ══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════                                   ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   0-2 months   Crying, cooing                      "Ahhh" "Ooooo"                                                         ║
║                (Vegetative sounds)                                                                                         ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   2-6 months   Babbling begins                     "Babababa" "Dadadada"                                                  ║
║                (Consonant-vowel combos)            (Practicing sounds)                                                     ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   6-12 months  Canonical babbling                  "Mama" "Dada" "Baba"                                                   ║
║                (Language-specific sounds)          (Sounds narrow to native language)                                     ║
║                Comprehension begins                Responds to name                                                        ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   12-18 months First words!                        "Mama" "Ball" "Dog"                                                    ║
║                One-word stage                      (Holophrastic: one word = whole thought)                               ║
║                                                    "Ball!" = "I want the ball"                                             ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   18-24 months Two-word stage                      "More milk" "Daddy go"                                                 ║
║                Vocabulary explosion                "No sleep" "Big dog"                                                   ║
║                (50 → 200+ words)                   (Telegraphic speech)                                                   ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   2-3 years    Multi-word sentences                "I want cookie"                                                        ║
║                Grammar emerges                     "Doggy running"                                                         ║
║                Overgeneralization                  "I goed" "Two mouses"                                                  ║
║                                                    (Shows rule learning!)                                                 ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   3-4 years    Complex sentences                   "I think that cookie is good"                                          ║
║                Questions, negations                "Why you did that?"                                                    ║
║                ~1000 words                         "I don't want no vegetables"                                            ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   4-5 years    Near-adult grammar                  "If I had a dinosaur, I would..."                                      ║
║                Narratives                          Tells stories with structure                                            ║
║                Metalinguistic awareness            Can talk about language itself                                          ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   5-10 years   Refinement                          Idioms, metaphors, sarcasm                                             ║
║                Literacy (reading/writing)          Phonics → Fluent reading                                                ║
║                Continued vocabulary growth         Academic language                                                       ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║   10+ years    Advanced discourse                  Persuasion, rhetoric, nuance                                           ║
║                Multiple styles                     Formal vs. informal registers                                           ║
║                Lifelong learning                   New words, new meanings                                                 ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║                                                                                                                            ║
║  Key Principles:                                                                                                           ║
║  • Universal: All children in normal environment acquire language                                                          ║
║  • Rapid: From no words to complex grammar in ~5 years                                                                     ║
║  • Effortless: Children don't "study"—it just happens                                                                      ║
║  • Creative: Generate novel sentences never heard before                                                                   ║
║  • Poverty of stimulus: Learn more than input seems to provide                                                             ║
║  • Critical period: Easier before puberty                                                                                  ║
